Isaw

  • Step 1: Clean the intestines thoroughly.
  • Step 2: Boil with spices and marinade.
  • Step 3: Skewer and grill to perfection.
  • Step 4: Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.

History of Isaw

Isaw, a popular street food in the Philippines, is made from skewered and grilled chicken or pork intestines. Often sold by street vendors, it's marinated, skewered, and grilled to a savory perfection. Isaw represents the creativity and resourcefulness of Filipino culinary tradition. It's often enjoyed with a cold beer or soft drink and is a staple during gatherings and celebrations.
