KweKwek

  • Quail eggs
  • Flour
  • Annatto powder or food coloring
  • Oil
Steps:
  1. Boil and peel eggs.
  2. Mix flour and coloring.
  3. Coat eggs with mixture.
  4. Fry until golden.

History of KweKwek

Kwek-kwek is a Filipino snack made of quail eggs that are coated in an orange-tinted batter and then deep-fried until crispy. They're usually served with a spicy vinegar sauce or a sweet, spicy sauce. Sometimes, they're also paired with other street foods like fish balls and squid balls.
